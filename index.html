Idaho

Idaho is a great state with natural beauty. There is wildlife to see and natural beauty to behold. It is located between Oregon and Montana. Idaho is known for its potatoes. Much of Idaho has embraced this fact and has many things to represent this such as a potato museum, potato hotel, and a dessert that looks like a potato!
