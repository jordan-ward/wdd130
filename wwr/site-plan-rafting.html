Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information wanted by the user in an easy and clean way that is pleasing to look at and navigate through.

Audience

What do they want? They will want a website that can be easy to navigate and plan a trip with ease. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The guests will be wanting to know that to expect while rafting, how much it is, how to sign up and reserve a spot. They would also possibly want to know about the guides and the business itself if it can be trusted. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Guests will most likely be accessing through a laptop or a mobile device. The mobile device would be the first option to keep in mind.
